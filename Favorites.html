My Favorites

Most best color in my opinion. The shades of pink attracts my eye and literally my favorite color of all.

One of my top #1 favorite and cravings all day. The reason why I like fries is because of the tastiness of the fried potato, the crispiness on the outside and soft on the inside, with a good balance of salt and seasonings.


Halo-halo

My favorite dessert because it's cold and refreshing especially on hot weather. My favorite part of the halo-halo is the ube ice cream.



Mango Float

It has layers of cream, crackers, and juicy ripe mangoes. It makes it more delicious to eat because of it's unique flavor, color, and the smell of grahams.



Tortang Talong

My favorite breakfast of all!



Mang Inasal

The free unlimited rice, tenderness and juicy of the chicken. One of my favorite filipino dining meal.



Milk Tea

The taste of the milk makes it more unique and most of all the pearls.




Thrift Store

One of my favorite places to go. Thrifting can help you save up for money and most of all you can find a lot of good branded clothes there which is very worth it to go.



Mall

Going on the mall or shopping with your family is the most thrilling thing.
